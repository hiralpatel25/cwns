Tabs

The tabs across the top of the page allow the Local User to navigate among the data areas that are relevant for the selected facility/project.  Tabs that are not applicable to the facility/project are not shown.  For example, NPS projects do not have the tabs Population, Discharge, and Utility Management.  The current tab is blue; all other tabs are tan (Figure 4-3).  

 

• After a facility/project is selected, move to a different data area by clicking on the desired tab.
