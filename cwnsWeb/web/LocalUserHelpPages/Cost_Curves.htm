Cost Curves

In some cases, the State can use Cost Curves to estimate the Capital Costs of documented needs.  They are only used when documented costs are not available.  Cost Curves are available to State Users in the DEP for the following Facility Types:

 

• Treatment Plant

 

• Clustered Systems (Decentralized Wastewater Treatment Systems)

 

• Onsite Wastewater Treatment Systems (Decentralized Wastewater Treatment Systems)

 

• Collection: Combined Sewers
