Areas Related to Needs

The Areas Related to Needs data area provides the Local User the opportunity to associate other geographic data, including County(ies), Congressional District(s), Watershed(s), and Boundary(ies),with the facility/project (Figure 6-7).

 

 

Figure 6- 7: Areas Related to Needs Data Area

 

 

 

The Primary County/ Congressional District/ Watershed is the one in which the majority or plurality of the needs, by dollar amount, occur.  The Primary County/ Congressional District/ Watershed is indicated by a check in the Primary column of the data table.
