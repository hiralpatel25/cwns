Create New Facility/Project

Ask WATERS Reports

 

Ask WATERS is tool to query data from CWNS at the facility/project, county, watershed, Congressional District, State, EPA region, and national level.  Ask WATERS Simple Query Tool provides nine simple questions related to CWNS:

 

1. How many CWNS 2004 facilities and projects have needs and in what categories are their needs?

 

2. What CWNS 2004 facilities and projects have needs documented to federal standards?

 

3. What are the current and projected flows for publicly owned wastewater treatment facilities?

 

4. How many people are served by publicly owned wastewater treatment facilities?

 

5. Combination of Questions #2 and #3

 

6. Combination of Questions #2 and #4

 

7. Combination of Questions #2, #3, and #4

 

8. What are the discharge methods and locations for CWNS 2004 facilities and projects?

 

9. What are the names and locations of publicly owned wastewater treatment facilities currently in operation?

 

It also provides two simple queries that compare data from CWNS with data from EPA's Impaired Waters Program:

 

1. What CWNS 2004 facilities and projects, with needs documented to federal standards, are located on impaired waters?

 

2. How many and what categories of CWNS 2004 facilities and projects, documented to federal standards, are located on impaired waters?

 

The Ask WATERS Expert Query Tool allows users to select key data elements to build a customized tabular report or a Common Separated Value (CSV) file for downloading.

 

• Click Ask WATERS on the Main Menu portlet to launch a pop-up window showing the Ask WATERS tool on the EPA website (http://www.epa.gov/waters/tools/ask_waters/index.html).  Queries are currently available for CWNS 2000 and 2004 data.
