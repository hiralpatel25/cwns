Facility/Project Menu

The Facility/Project Menu (Figure 7-1) provides links to the:

 

• Facility/Project Fact Sheet containing a summary of the most recent data entered for the facility/project

 

• Small Community Needs Form for facility/project’s with a total population of fewer than 10,000 persons (see Chapter 15)

 

• Feedback Fact Sheet when a Local User has provided feedback (see Chapter15)

 

• Click to launch the HTML version and to launch the pdf version the Facility/Project Fact Sheet, Small Community Needs Form, or Feedback Fact Sheet.
