Treatment Plant Cost Curves

For facilities with the Type “Treatment Plant,” the available Cost Curves are dependent on the Change Type (Table 9-5).

Only one of the following Treatment Plant Cost Curves may be assigned to a facility:

 

• Increase Flow Capacity

 

• Increase Level of Treatment

 

• Increase Flow Capacity and Level of Treatment

 

• New Treatment Plant

 

• Replacement of Treatment Plant

 

• Disinfection Only

 

If one of these is already assigned to the facility, all other Cost Curves in the list above are disabled.
