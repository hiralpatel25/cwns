NPDES Permit Search

The NPDES Permit Search allows users to perform a keyword search on all the NPDES permits for the user’s State.  The system searches for the keyword in the Permit Number, County, and Facility Name data fields.

 

• Enter a keyword or phrase in the keyword box.  Click to begin the search (Figure 7-6).  

 

The first five Permits that meet the search criteria are displayed in the portlet.  The total number of Permits is indicated at the bottom of the portlet (e.g., 1-5 of 76).  To navigate the search results, click Next to display the next five Permits for the search criteria (if not at the end of the list) and click Prev to display the previous five Permits (if not at the beginning of the list).  Click >> to display the last page of the Permits list and << to display the first page of the Permits list.  If no Permits meet the search criteria, “No records found” is displayed.

 

The search results display the Permit Number, Facility Type, Permit Type, and County.  They also indicates if the permit is “Associated with Other Facility(s).”  Permits already assigned to the current facility/project are not displayed.

 

• Click the hyperlinked Permit Number to open a pop-up box with additional NPDES Permit Information, including:

 

• NPDES Permit Number

 

• A box indicating whether or not the permit is Active

 

• NPDES Facility Name

 

• Receiving Waters

 

• City

 

• Latitude and Longitude

 

• Location Description

 

• Scale

 

• Method

 

• Datum

 

• When applicable, a list of the other Facilities Associated with Permit. The CWNS number, Facility Name, Review Status, and Use Data status are indicated for each facility

 

This is the data used to populate the Coordinates, Facility Address, and Primary County fields when the “Use Data” box is checked for the permit.

 

• To associate the Permit(s) with the facility/project, select the box(es) of the desired Permit(s) in the NPDES Search Portlet and click .  To use the data from a selected permit, click  in the Edit column to open the permit and check box “Use Data.”
