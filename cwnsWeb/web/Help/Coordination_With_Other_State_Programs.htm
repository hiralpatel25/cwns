Coordination With Other State Programs

EPA encourages states to coordinate with other State programs in order to better document needs across all categories.  State Stormwater, Nonpoint Source (NPS), Onsite and Decentralized Treatment, Agriculture, and Rural Services staffs can provide valuable information and documentation about their programs.  Contact information for State NPS, Decentralized, and Stormwater Coordinators is available at http://www.epa.gov/cwns/cwns2008.htm under Resources.

 

CWNS coordinators can grant access to State staff to update and add facilities/projects directly within the DEP.  See Chapter 6 for more information.
