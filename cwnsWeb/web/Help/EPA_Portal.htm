EPA Portal

Resources for State and Regional Coordinators are located in the EPA Portal (portal.epa.gov) (Figure 3-1).  The EPA Portal provides access to a variety of resources including the EPA Collaboration Site, Library, and Web Conferencing tool.  See Chapter 4 for more information about the Portal.

 

To establish a CWNS Portal Community account:

 

• State or Regional CWNS Coordinators must send an e-mail to cwns@epa.gov requesting that State and Regional staff be added to the CWNS Portal Community.

 

• Go to http://portal.epa.gov and choose "Request access to the EPA Portal."  Complete the self registration page, using 'Michael Plastino' (e-mail address plastino.michael@epa.gov) as the EPA sponsor. Choose "General Portal Access."  Note:  EPA employees automatically have Portal accounts (User Name = LAN id; Password = LAN password).

 

After EPA’s CWNS team approves the CWNS Portal Community account, it provides access to the CWNS Portal Library and Collaboration Site.
