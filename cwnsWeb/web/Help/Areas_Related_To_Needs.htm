Areas Related To Needs

The Areas Related to Needs data area provides the user the opportunity to associate other geographic data, including County(ies), Congressional District(s), Watershed(s), and Boundary(ies), with the facility/project (Figure 8-7).

 

One and only one Primary County, Congressional District, and Watershed is required for the facility/project.  The Primary County/ Congressional District/ Watershed is the one in which the majority or plurality of the needs, by dollar amount, occur.  The latitude and longitude entered in the Coordinates data area must be within five miles of the Primary County/ Congressional District/ Watershed.  The Primary County/ Congressional District/ Watershed is indicated by a check in the Primary column of the data table.
