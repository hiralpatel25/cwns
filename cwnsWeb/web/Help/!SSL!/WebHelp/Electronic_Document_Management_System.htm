Electronic Document Management System (EDMS)

The EDMS enables CWNS 2008 DEP users to upload documents, annotate them to indicate portions of documents relevant to submitted data, and share documents from one facility/project to another (Chapter 9).  EMDS also enables the ability to received marked up small community forms as faxes, convert them to Adobe Portable Document Files (PDFs), and connect them with relevant facilities/projects within the DEP (Chapter 15).
