CWNS 2008 Workgroup Contributions

In response to an August 2005 letter to Environmental Council of the States (ECOS) Commissioners and EPA’s Regional Water Division Directors, the CWNS Workgroup convened in September 2005.  The Workgroup had active participation from approximately thirty States and six EPA Regions.  Detailed input and recommendations generally took place within the four subcommittees (Figure 1-1), with the Workgroup providing broader input and cross-cutting perspectives on issues.  Appendix A summarizes subcommittee and workgroup topics and participants.
