Coordination With Local Users

Local Users, such as wastewater treatment plant managers, municipal officials, local health departments, universities, non-governmental organizations, and conservation districts, are a valuable source of data for CWNS.  EPA encourages states to reach out to Local Users and provides the following resources to assist states in that effort:

 

• CWNS coordinators can grant access to State staff to update and add facilities/projects directly within the DEP.  See Chapters 6 and 15 for more information.

 

• EPA has developed a Small Community Survey to collect data from communities with fewer than 10,000 persons.  Two types of Small Community Surveys are available: (1) a blank form; (2) a form populated with the most current CWNS data available in the DEP (CWNS 2004 data with subsequent updates completed by State) (see CWNS Portal Library in this Chapter and Chapter 15).

 

• EPA has developed a survey for collecting Decentralized Wastewater Treatment data (see CWNS Portal Library in this Chapter).
