Roles and Responsibilities

EPA Headquarters

 

Overall CWNS implementation

 

• Ensure national consistency by establishing sound policies and procedures

 

• Approve development and ensure implementation of the Quality Assurance Project Plan (QAPP)

 

• Manage contractors

 

• Prepare final Report to Congress (RtC)

 

• Act as final arbitrator in disputes

 

• Manage and control security requirements

 

Management of the CWNS DEP and database

 

• Coordinate with EPA/RTP to ensure a proper environment for the DEP is maintained

 

• Coordinate DEP training sessions and videos

 

• Ensure DEP concerns are prioritized and resolved as resources allow

 

• Perform independent testing of DEP

 

Provide CWNS administrative and technical support

 

• Provide user support to states and locals (with assistance of Regions) on issues related to the DEP

 

Regions

 

Assist EPA headquarters in Regional management of the CWNS 2008

 

• Ensure States are documenting needs, through whatever means necessary (e.g., phone calls, e-mails, visits to State offices)

 

• Emphasize to States all the various uses of CWNS data

 

• Communicate with States on data submission status and milestones

 

• Assist EPA Headquarters with data dispute resolution

 

• Provide and/or coordinate mentoring for novice State Coordinators

 

States

 

Update, review, and submit CWNS data

 

• Collect and enter State data for all needs categories

 

• Analyze current data;  update cost, technical information, and documentation

 

• Collect and enter data on new facilities

 

• Submit facility/project documentation to contractor

 

• Adhere to schedule for updating facilities data

 

• Address questions regarding data anomalies

 

• Comply with local level security requirements

 

• Address CWNS 2008 priorities

 

• Provide other State staff, facilities, municipalities, and others access to the DEP to submit their data electronically to the State  

 

Contractor

 

Analyze State-supplied CWNS data

 

• Develop QAPP

 

• Review State-supplied data to ensure their accuracy

 

• Ensure national consistency through established documentation procedures

 

• Perform quality assurance checks on needs, population, and other technical data

 

• Analyze needs estimates at the end of data collection

 

• Prepare draft CWNS Report

 

Provide CWNS administrative and technical support

 

• Assist EPA in maintaining DEP and correcting programming errors

 

• Provide advice to EPA Headquarters and guidance to Regions on technical issues involving the CWNS

 

• Support CWNS meetings (briefings, start-up, mid-survey, etc.)

 

• Implement EPA policy
