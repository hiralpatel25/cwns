Summary of CWNS Workgroup Input on 2008 Methods

In response to an August 2005 letter to Environmental Council of the States (ECOS) Commissioners, the CWNS Workgroup convened in September 2005.  The Workgroup had active participation from approximately thirty States and six EPA Regions (Table A-1).  Detailed input and recommendations generally took place within the four subcommittees (Figure A-1), with the Workgroup providing broader input and cross-cutting perspectives on issues.

 

 

Figure A-1: Relationship of CWNS Workgroup & Subcommittees

 

 

 

Recommendations from DSARMS and CIS were presented to and deliberated upon by the CWNS Workgroup.  The CWNS Workgroup voted on each recommendation.  Voting results are presented in Tables A-2 and A-3.

Given the high level of detail related to WSS input on the DEP, instead of the Workgroup voting on each WSS item of input, the input was periodically reviewed with the Workgroup to enable broader workgroup input on the DEP and to consider DEP input in relationship with DSARMS and SLIS activities.

 

SLIS formed in March 2006, following the input provided by five local facility representatives at the WSS finalization of system requirements.  The SLIS was formed to focus on State-Local interaction in the DEP as well as to provide input on how to make the Report to Congress, CWNS data on the Internet/fact sheets, and outreach materials more relevant to local communities.

 

The CIS provided high level recommendations on priorities for functions needed from other related systems such as EDMS and WLV.  Following completion of CIS work in February 2006, some members joined the WSS and some joined the SLIS.

 

The following pages present the names of Workgroup participants, charters for each of the subcommittees, and vote results (Table A-2 and A-3).  Vote results, along with WSS and CIS system input and system documentation, are available in the CWNS Portal Library.  (See Chapter 2 for information on how to access this resource.)

Data Submission and Review Methods Subcommittee (DSARMS) Charter

 

Timelines for below topics are overlapping, to allow introduction of a second topic to occur during periods where first topic discussion is on hold for research and analysis.  

 

• Small Facility sampling investigation & documentation rules:  October 05 - February 06

• 20-year needs methodology:  October 05 - February 06

• NPS documentation rules:  March 06 - June 06

• Cost curve assessment & CSO documentation:  April 06 - September

• Review/Update Rules on which/how Doc Types can be used for Needs, Costs:  May 06 – June 06

• Review/Update Categories & Subcategories used to track needs:  June 06 – July 06

• Redocumentation and Footnotable documentation rules:  August 06 – November 06

• Review process:  December 06 – February 07

• Ad-hoc Analysis of Web System Subcommittee questions:  October 05 – January 08
