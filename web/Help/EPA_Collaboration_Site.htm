EPA Collaboration Site

Access to the EPA Collaboration Site is given to State and Regional CWNS staff; local user resources are provided on the EPA website.

 

• Enter the portal at http://portal.epa.gov .  Under the heading General Pages, click Team Resources).  The Team Resources page includes access the Collaborative Workspace (Figure 3-2.)

 

• Click Enter Collaborative Workspace.  This opens the Collaboration Suite Workspaces.  Workspaces to which the user has membership are listed (Figure 3-3).

 

• Click CWNS.  This opens the CWNS area and provides access to the CWNS Portal Library.

 

Direct links to the EPA Collaboration Site and the Library are available on the Main Menu portlet in the DEP (see Chapter 5).
