Small Community Form

A Small Community Form is available in the DEP for all communities with total population fewer than 10,000 persons (Figure 15-5).  It is populated with the most current facility/project data available in the DEP. The form includes a fax cover sheet and instructions to complete the form. EPA recommends that States add a cover letter explaining the importance of participating in CWNS, setting a deadline for returning the form, and providing other State-specific information.

 

The Small Community Form is launched from the Facility/Project Menu on the Facility/Project Information tab (Figure 15-2).

 

• Click to launch the HTML version and to launch the PDF version of the Small Community Form.

 

Small communities return the cover sheet and form via the fax number printed on the cover sheet.  The fax cover sheet includes a bar code which allows the receiving fax to associate the Small Community Form with the proper facility/project.  The State User is notified of the arrival of the fax via the Announcements portlet on the CWNS 2008 Welcome page (Figure 15-4; Chapter 5).

 

The Small Community Survey is automatically listed in and uploaded to the correct facility/project’s Needs by Document data area (Figure 15-6; Chapter 9).  The document is listed under the title “Small Community Form- facility/project name,” author “fax,” and Type 71.  The Publish Date and Base Date are the date that the fax arrived.  Remember: The base date needs to be edited based on the information provided by the Small Community.

 

 

Figure 15- 4: The Announcements Portlet Indicates the Arrival of a Small Community Survey Via Fax.

 

 

 

A blank EPA-approved Small Community Needs Form is available in the CWNS Portal Library (see Chapter 3).  The blank form cannot be returned via the fax system explained above.

 

Resources to assist small communities complete both forms are available on EPA’s CWNS 2008 web pages (http://www.epa.gov/cwns/cwns2008.htm).
