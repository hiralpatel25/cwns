Documentation Criteria Review

Documents are reviewed to ensure they meet the seven documentation criteria (see Chapter 2 for details):

 

1. A description of the water quality or public health problem or threat.  

 

2. The location of the problem.

 

3. The solution to the problem.

 

4. The cost for each solution.  

 

5. The basis of the cost.  

 

6. The total cost.  

 

7. Current documentation

 

Note: Submitted documents (and their corresponding needs) not complying with the annotation guidelines outlined in Chapter 9 will not be reviewed.  States will be informed via e-mail if a document does not comply with the instructions and will be asked to resubmit the document.
