Monthly Data Validation Checks

Beginning October 1, 2008,  the EPA contractor starts monthly reviews of facilities/projects with the Review Status “Federal Review Requested” to:

 

• Ensure all needs data submitted meet CWNS eligibility criteria

 

• Check technical data for logical soundness

 

• Verify the consistency between data entered in DEP and data in submitted documents

 

States are encouraged to make ad hoc requests for reviews if they have a certain type of facility/project or type of data that they want feedback on prior to entering successive facilities/projects.  They are also encouraged to provide advanced communication when submitting documents for complicated or previously problematic facilities.

 

After the EPA contractor reviews a facility, he/she indicates the result of the review using the Review Status and Review Comments functions within the DEP.  He/she changes the Review Status of the facility/project to either:

 

• State Correction Requested (formerly Headquarter Rejected/HR) when the data does not meet all the CWNS eligibility and documentation criteria.

 

• Federal Accepted (formerly Headquarters Accepted/HA) when the data is found to be acceptable.

 

When the EPA contractor assigns the State Correction Requested status, additional information about needed changes may be provided in the Review Comments Portlet for the facility/project (Figure 17-1).  Data areas that need correction are indicated by a check mark in the Cor column of the Summary portlet.  After addressing comments, the State changes the Status to Federal Review Correction.

 

If a facility/project does not meet all data and documentation criteria by the data correction deadline (March 20, 2009) the facility/project will remain at State Correction Requested and its needs are considered Unofficial and included in the Report to Congress appendices.

 

If a State does not agree with repeated State Correction Requested designations for a particular facility/project, the State may appeal by sending an email to cwns@epa.gov within thirty days of the status assignment date during the data entry period, or within five business days during the post collection data correction period (February 28-March 20, 2009).

 

Additional information about Review Statuses is available in Chapter 2 and the Review Comments portlet is available in Chapter 5.
