Decentralized Wastewater Treatment Cost

For Decentralized Wastewater Treatment Cost Curves, the available Cost Curves are dependent on the Facility Type and Change Type (Table 9-6).

 

Additionally, the following rules apply:

 

• If the Cost Curve “New Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – All” is assigned to a facility, the Cost Curves “New Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – innovative” and “New Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – conventional” cannot be assigned.  They are disabled in the DEP.

 

• If either the Cost Curve “New Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – innovative” or “New Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – conventional” is assigned, the Cost Curve “New Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – all” cannot be assigned and is disabled in the DEP.

 

• If the Cost Curve “Rehabilitate Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – all” is assigned, the Cost Curves “Rehabilitate Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – innovative” and “Rehabilitate Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – conventional” cannot be assigned and are disabled in the DEP.

 

• If either the Cost Curve “Rehabilitate Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – innovative” or “Rehabilitate Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – conventional” is assigned, the Cost Curve “Rehabilitate Onsite Wastewater Treatment System – all” cannot be assigned and is disabled in the DEP.
