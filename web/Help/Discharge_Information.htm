Discharge Information

The data area Discharge Information displays all discharges currently assigned to the facility/project.  Users can view, edit, and add discharge information for the following Facility Types:

 

• Confined Animals (Point Source)

 

• Mining (Point Source)

 

• Treatment Lagoon or Pond

 

• Storage Facility

 

• Collection: Pump Stations

 

• Phase I MS4

 

• Phase II MS4

 

• Non-traditional MS4

 

• Unregulated Community

 

• Collection: Separate Sewers

 

• Collection: Combined Sewers

 

• Collection: Interceptor Sewer

 

• Treatment Plant

 

• Recycled Water Distribution

 

• Individual On-site System Area

 

• Decentralized Wastewater Treatment System

 

Remember: A warning message and the Feedback Status “Local Assigned” indicate that a Feedback copy exists for this facility/project.  When a Feedback copy exists, any changes made to the data area are reflected on the Feedback copy.  If a feedback copy has been returned (Feedback Status “State Review Requested”), this symbol indicates that a Feedback Fact Sheet includes changes to this data area.  Click it to view a pop-up box containing Feedback Fact Sheet.

 

• Click Add Discharge to add a new Discharge Method to the facility/project.  Clickin the Edit column to edit an existing Discharge Method.  Both actions open the Add/Edit Discharge Method data area (Figure 11-1).  Explanations of the fields in this data area are in Table 11-1.  

 

• Click in the Delete column to remove a Discharge Method from the list.

Table 11- 2: Explanations of Warning Messages for the Discharge Data Area

 

Warning Message

Explanation

Present Discharge to Surface Water is Less than Secondary

Displayed when the present Effluent is Raw Discharge/Primary/Advanced Primary and the present Discharge method is Ocean Discharge, Outfall to Surface Waters, or Overland Flow with Discharge exist.

Displayed when the projected Effluent is Raw Discharge/Primary/Advanced Primary and the projected Discharge method of Ocean Discharge, Outfall to Surface Waters, or Overland Flow with Discharge exist.

Displayed when the present Discharge Method is Ocean Discharge, Outfall to Surface Waters, or Overland Flow is selected, and the present Facility Type of Treatment Plant is not associated with the facility.

Displayed when the projected Discharge method of Ocean Discharge, Outfall to Surface Waters, or Overland Flow is selected and a projected Facility Type of Treatment Plant is not associated with the facility.
