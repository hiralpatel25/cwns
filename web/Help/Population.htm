Population

The data area Population (Figure 10-1) is enabled for facilities with the following Facility/Project Types: Collection: Separate Sewers, Collection: Combined Sewers, Treatment Plant, Onsite Wastewater Treatment System, Clustered System, Treatment Lagoon or Pond, Storage Facility, and Collection: Pump Stations.

 

 

Figure 10- 1: Population Information Data Area

 

 

 

For this data area:

 

• “Resident Population” applies to people who live within the service area of the selected facility.

 

• “Non-resident Population” applies to people who do not live within the service area of the facility but still use or are served by the associated sewers, treatment plants, or decentralized treatment systems.  This population includes transient, seasonal, or commuting workers and tourists.

 

• “Present Population” refers to the population currently associated with the facility.

 

• “Projected Population” refers to the population that will be associated with the facility in the design year.

 

The Population Information data fields are described in Table 10-1.  Add/Edit Onsite Wastewater Treatment Systems and Clustered Systems Population data fields are described in Table 10-2.

 

Remember: A warning message and the Feedback Status “Local Assigned” indicate that a Feedback copy exists for this facility/project.  When a Feedback copy exists, any changes made to the data area are reflected on the Feedback copy.  If a feedback copy has been returned (Feedback Status “State Review Requested”), this symbol indicates that a Feedback Fact Sheet includes changes to this data area.  Click it to view a pop-up box containing Feedback Fact Sheet.

Small Community Exception Flag

Check this box if the facility’s population is fewer than 10,000 persons and meets one of the following:

    • One of several facilities serving a community of 10,000 persons or more.

 

    • Its wastewater system and its location within an urbanized area make it an component of, and virtually indistinguishable from, surrounding or adjacent entities.

 

    • Its wastewater system is physically connected to a regional treatment authority serving 10,000 persons or more.

 

When this box is checked, the facility is not considered a small community for the purposes of CWNS.
