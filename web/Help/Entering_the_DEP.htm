Entering the DEP

Users can enter the DEP through either the CWNS 2008 website (http://www.epa.gov/cwns/cwns2008.htm) or via the EPA Portal site (portal.epa.gov).  Before going to either site, close all open Internet browsers.  Start the portal login with a new Internet browser.

 

• At either site, enter the portal user name and password to login.

 

• After logging in the EPA Portal Site, the user lands at the EPA Portal Welcome Page.  Select CWNS (on the left hand menu) to enter the DEP.

 

Note: Internet Explorer 7.0 or higher is recommended.  Users with Internet Explorer 6.0 cannot launch the calendar function; they can enter dates manually.
